I'm passionate about making science accessible to everyone by ensuring that all have the resources and opportunities they need to get involved. Here are some of my favourite outreach initiatives I've taken part in.

  • CUWiP UK

    Served on the Local Organising Committee for the Conference for Undergraduate Women in Physics (CUWiP) UK at the University of Southampton in 2021, helping to create an inclusive and supportive environment for women in physics.

  • Field of View

    Hosted Physics Chat on the YouTube channel Field of View, a series of accessible conversations about physics research aimed at making science approachable for everyone.

  • Royal Society Summer Science Exhibition

    Engaged with the public at JWST's First Years of Discovery during the 2024 Royal Society Summer Science Exhibition, showcasing the latest science from the James Webb Space Telescope.

  • Build a Mini JWST

    Designed a hands-on "Build a Mini JWST" craft activity to bring the excitement of space science to all ages. The activity is now featured at both Portsmouth Stargazing and Portsmouth Comic Con.

  • Astronomy on Tap NYC

    Delivered a public talk at Astronomy on Tap NYC, bringing accessible astronomy to a general audience in an informal setting.

If you're interested in any of my research or outreach and would like to chat further, please do get in touch!
