Faculty of Tech Conference

In June 2024 I attended the University of Portsmouth's Faculty of Tech conference, presenting my research to those within my faculty. I worked hard to make my poster accessible to those outside of physics whilst still describing the process of simulating emission lines in detail. I really enjoyed the process of designing my poster, titled 'Generating Synthetic Observations for the James Webb Space Telescope', and I appreciate each opportunity to improve my skills in scientific communication.
